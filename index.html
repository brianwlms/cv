Brian A. Williams

Senior Executive Vice President, Ramsey+

Brian Williams is a Ramsey Solutions Operating Board member and serves as the Senior Executive Vice President of Ramsey+,
an all-access membership to Ramsey’s bestselling money products like Financial Peace and EveryDollar. He joined the team in
2006 as a graphic designer, eventually leading and growing the Creative Team to over 100 people before transitioning to the
business side of the company. There, he built and launched the Business Boutique brand—a podcast, book and conference designed
to help women make money doing what they love. Brian then went on to lead the team that launched the world-class budgeting app,
EveryDollar. Today, as the SEVP of Ramsey Plus, Brian has been instrumental in transitioning Ramsey Solutions flagship product,
FPU, to a fully digital experience.
